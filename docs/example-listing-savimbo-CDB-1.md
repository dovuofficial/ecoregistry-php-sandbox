### Description

This project applies the Savimbo Indicator Species Biodiversity Methodology to strengthen conservation efforts in a high-biodiversity area within the Colombian Amazon. Villagarzón, Putumayo borders the Tropical Andean biodiversity hotspot, local conservationists have preserved this biozone without resources and against external influence for greater than twenty years. The BCP offers a streamlined approach to safeguarding the local ecosystem, strengthening existing local conservation networks, and mitigating biodiversity loss. The BCP also enables sustainable livelihoods for local experts and youth in biodiversity monitoring, conservation practices, and technical forestry.
